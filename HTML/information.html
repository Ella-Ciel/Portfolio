ABOUT ME

My interest in Programming and passion in Writing

My interest in technology took root in junior high, where I began developing my foundational skills in web development using HTML, CSS, and JS. This early exposure inspired me to pursue a degree in Information Technology to further professionalize my technical abilities. Beyond the screen, I am a dedicated novelist and avid reader—a passion I've nurtured since elementary school. Writing serves as my primary outlet for self-expression, allowing me to bring a creative perspective to my technical problem-solving.

The meaning behind C1E7 710AE and Cielies

C1E7 71A0E is the encoded signature of Ciel Liove, a name I crafted during my final year of senior high school. 'Ciel' is the French word for sky, while 'Liove' is a fusion of Life and Love. Together, they represent my philosophy: no matter the condition of the sky, I will always admire its beauty. I associate the sky with life because they share the same unpredictable nature—sometimes peaceful, sometimes masking a hidden storm, and at other times, seemingly cruel. Yet, I cherish these shifts because they have taught me the values I carry today. For my projects, I often use the nickname Cielies—a phonetic play on the abbreviation 'CLL'—to carry the spirit of Ciel Liove into everything I build.
